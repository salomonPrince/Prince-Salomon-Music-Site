About

Welcome to the site of your artist Prince Salomon, you will find everything you want on me, particularly my different musical works, and everything else.
Prince Salomon is an African Musician Artist who lives in Canada Montreal. Born in 1996 on 4 June in the Democratic Republic of the Congo, he left for Rwanda at the age of 8. He studied elementary and high school before leaving Africa for Canada, Began his music career in 2020 following the pandemic until today.

  • Read Bio

  • Prince salomon are a 1-piece Rwandese/Congolese Afrobeat band from Montreal, Quebec. The Artist band formed in 2021 from a collection of friend's ideas, hard working, having a great vision and energy, Prince talent started in High school And then he started singing songs from other artists. just couldn't get enough until he started his carrer. .

    He's unique brand of entertainment fuses Afro music and Dancehall with full Live performance and karaoke sometime. makes people dance, smile and forget about stress
